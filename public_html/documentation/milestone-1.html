Purpose

Since my PWP is going to be my own personal professional portfolio I would like to use it as a resource for potential employers to find me and to learn more about my experience and skills. This professional portfolio will demonstrate my web development skills and where I can talk about the experience I have gained as an intern at Deep Dive Full Stack.

Audience

My audience for this project will be potential employers and/or hiring personal including; recruiters, HR personal, hiring managers and tech leads. I would like to attract this audience on both a local and national scope.

Goal

The goal of my project is to help me acquire an internship or gainful employment. I would like to be employed full time but any experience I can gain from this project will be very beneficial on my journey to become a full stack web developer.

Persona

Greg Johnson

Age 30

Technology

  • Dell Inspiron 15 running Window's 10 Home Edition, Samsung Galaxy S21 Ultra running Android 10
  • Proficiency: Power user, Greg frequently uses his computer for work. He also has a background in coding and web development.
  • Passion for technology: Because of his technical background, Greg is very passionate about technology and does his best to stay up to date on the latest and greatest in the industry.

Attitudes and Needs

Greg is a technical recruiter constantly on the hunt for new tech talent. He will be using my professional portfolio to assess my programming skills to see if I am the right fit for some jobs that have recently opened up in town. My website should clearly state my training and skills as well as have links to my projects and contact information.

Frustrations

Portfolios with no link to the potential recruit's github or LinkedIn.

Portfolios without up to date contact information.

Messy website layouts that are confusing to navigate.

User Story

As a user I need to clearly assess a candidate's skills to see if they are the right fit for the jobs I've been hired to fill.
