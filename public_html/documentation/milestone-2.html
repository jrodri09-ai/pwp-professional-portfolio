My professional portfolio will be one page and consist of the following sections.

Welcome

A one-line introduction with my name and title as a full stack developer. The background image behind this introduction will be a New Mexican sunset.

About Me

In this section I will speak about my professional skills as well as my education and relevant prior experience. Underneath the paragraph talking about myself I will place a list that clearly states the different programming languages I know so the employer or client can determine easily if I am the right fit for the job.

Portfolio

This section will be an image gallery of my projects that the user can click on in order to more closely inspect my work. This section will contain 4 or 5 of my projects.

Contact

This section will include a contact form and links to my LinkedIn and GitHub accounts. The links for my LinkedIn and GitHub will be in the footer.
